## DAY-BY-DAY ITINERARY

### Day 1 — Arrive & Settle In
**City:** Sarajevo
**Summary:** Transfer, welcome coffee, old town orientation, and a home-cooked dinner with your hosts.

**Morning:**
Your journey begins with a private transfer from Sarajevo Airport or the bus station directly to your accommodation. Check-in is available from 2pm — bags down, breath taken, city ahead.

**Afternoon:**
Your guide meets you for a welcome coffee — on us — before leading you through the first steps of the old town. This is not a rushed sightseeing sprint. It is an orientation walk that introduces Sarajevo properly, sets the tone for everything ahead, and answers every question you didn't know you had yet.

**Evening:**
Dinner is hosted in a local Sarajevo home — homemade, generous, and nothing like a restaurant. A table full of food, a family that knows how to welcome guests, and a first evening in Bosnia that quietly sets the bar for everything that follows.

Over the meal, your guide will sit with you, talk through the days ahead, and ask what kind of Day 3 you want — so that by the time the table is cleared, the rest of the trip is already shaped around you.

**Meals:** Dinner with local family
**Accommodation:** 4* hotel, Sarajevo
**Included activities:** Private airport transfer · Sarajevo History Walking Tour · Home-hosted Dinner
**Optional activities:** —

### Day 2 — The Siege & the City
**City:** Sarajevo
**Summary:** A four-hour war tour, the Olympic bobsleigh track, cable car, and a free evening in Sarajevo.

**Morning:**
The day opens with Sarajevo's most detailed war and siege tour — four hours that cover the 1,425 days that defined this city. We will visit the Tunnel of Hope, the 800-metre lifeline dug beneath the besieged airport that kept Sarajevo alive; the tour moves through the museum, the siege viewpoints, and the stories that no guidebook captures the way a local guide does. This is the heaviest morning of the trip — and the most important one.

**Afternoon:**
The mood lifts with altitude. After lunch, the route heads up to the abandoned Olympic bobsleigh track on Trebević — built for the 1984 Winter Games, scarred by the war, and one of the most strangely beautiful sights in the city. The cable car carries you back down over a Sarajevo that looks, from above, entirely at peace.

**Evening:**
The evening is yours. Your guide will send you off with honest, personal recommendations — where locals actually eat, where to find the best bars and places to unwind after dark, and which corners of Baščaršija are worth one more slow walk before bed.

**Meals:** Breakfast at hotel · Lunch at local eatery
**Accommodation:** 4* hotel, Sarajevo
**Included activities:** Cable Car Ride · Sarajevo Eateries Experience · Sarajevo War Tour: Siege, Tunnel of Hope & Fall of Yugoslavia
**Optional activities:** Yellow Fortress sunset — 20 min walk from Baščaršija

### Day 3 — Your Day, Your Way
**City:** Sarajevo
**Summary:** A day built around your interests, closing with a farewell dinner at a local Sarajevo restaurant.

**Morning:**
When you book, our team will ask what kind of day you want: active or unhurried, nature or heritage, familiar or completely off the map. By this morning, the plan is already made and the day belongs entirely to you.

**Afternoon:**
You are able to choose from the following:

- **Trebević Summit Hike** — A trail to the top of the mountain that watched over Sarajevo through the centuries, ending at a mountain lodge above the treeline with views that earn every step.
- **Fojnica, Kozice Waterfalls & the Spring of the River Bosnia** — A drive through the town of Fojnica before reaching the Kozice waterfalls — a cascading series of falls tucked into the central Bosnian countryside that most travelers never find. The day ends at the spring of the River Bosnia, where the water emerges cold and clear.
- **Bijambare Nature Reserve & Cave** — A walk through one of Sarajevo's protected natural areas, leading into a small cave system that most visitors to the city never know exists.

**Evening:**
The trip closes the right way — around a table. Your farewell dinner is at one of Sarajevo's finest local restaurants, chosen by your guide for the quality of its kitchen and its distance from the tourist trail. A last evening in Bosnia worth staying up for.

**Meals:** Breakfast at the hotel
**Accommodation:** 4* hotel, Sarajevo
**Included activities:** Trebević Summit Hike · Umoljani & the Spring of the River Bosnia · Fojnica & Kozica Waterfalls
**Optional activities:** Lunch · Farewell Dinner

## WRITING NOTES FOR NEW PACKAGES
*(Read this before writing content for a new package)*

- **Tone:** Warm, confident, unhurried. First or second person. Never salesy.
- **About section:** Lead with the soul of the place, not a list of what's included. Make the reader feel something before they read a single logistic.
- **Day summaries:** One sentence. Factual but evocative — what happens and why it matters.
- **Morning/Afternoon/Evening:** Write as if you're a guide telling a friend what the day will be like. Specific details beat vague superlatives.
- **Activity descriptions:** One sentence each. What it is + what makes it worth doing.
- **Suitability:** Be honest. The "think twice" section builds trust — it tells guests you care about the right fit, not just the booking.
- **Important info:** Direct and practical. Guests read this when they're deciding whether to commit. No fluff.
